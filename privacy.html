Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 22, 2026

Wayfare stores your game progress and preferences locally on your device. If you use export or import, that backup remains under your control and is processed on your device.

The free version uses Google AdMob to show advertising. AdMob may process information such as device identifiers, IP address, advertising interactions, diagnostics, and approximate location to deliver, measure, prevent fraud, and improve ads. Where required, Wayfare asks for your consent before requesting ads and provides ad privacy choices in Settings. Wayfare Plus subscribers do not receive ad requests.

Purchases are processed by Apple and subscription status is managed through RevenueCat. Wayfare does not receive your full payment details. RevenueCat may process app user identifiers, purchase history, and diagnostics needed to provide and restore purchases.

These services operate under their own policies: Google Privacy Policy and RevenueCat Privacy Policy.

We do not sell personal information. You can reset the app's local data by deleting Wayfare. For privacy questions, use the developer support link on Wayfare's App Store page.
